Kardi.ru
Kardi.ru - is a new, promising project from Medical
Computer Systems. It gives everyone the opportunity
to independently control his heart in just one minute.
With this application you will be comfortable
to view the results of recent surveys, monitor the dynamics
of the state of the heart, to be notified of new surveys
and reports from the doctor to correct personal information
and even buy the device.
